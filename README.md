# Leiden Sociale Kaart
 
## Project Description
 
**Leiden Sociale Kaart** is a web application designed to help residents, social workers, and care professionals in the Leiden region discover and navigate local social support services and community resources.
 
The platform provides an interactive map-based overview of social amenities in Leiden, including:
 
- Community centres and neighbourhood hubs
- Mental health and wellbeing services
- Financial support and debt counselling
- Youth and family support services
- Elderly care and day activities
- Volunteer organisations and civic initiatives
- Education, language courses, and integration programmes
 
### Who is it for?
 
The application serves a broad audience:
 
- **Residents** looking for nearby support, activities, or services
- **Social workers and case managers** who need a quick reference to refer clients to the right organisations
- **Care professionals** seeking a comprehensive overview of non-medical support in the city
- **Volunteers and organisations** wanting to be visible and accessible to the community
 
### Why Leiden Sociale Kaart?
 
Leiden has a rich landscape of social organisations, but this information is often scattered, hard to find, or out of date. Leiden Sociale Kaart centralises this information into a single, accessible web application — making it easier to connect people with the help they need, when they need it.
